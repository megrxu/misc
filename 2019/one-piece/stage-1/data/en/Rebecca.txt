Rebecca is the former crown princess of Dressrosa, who forfeited her title to live with her father and currently serves as a lady-in-waiting to the royal family.
She was a gladiator at the Corrida Colosseum who joined a tournament to compete for the Mera Mera no Mi.
She is the granddaughter of Riku Doldo III, the daughter of Scarlett and Kyros, and the niece of Viola.
