Kozuki Toki, née Amatsuki (天月, Amatsuki), was the wife of the daimyo of Kuri, Kozuki Oden, and the mother of Momonosuke and Hiyori.
Born around 830 years before the present day, she spent her first 26 years jumping forward in time with her Devil Fruit ability.
After jumping to 30 years before the present, she met Oden and stayed with him for 10 years, until he was executed and she was killed during the destruction of Oden Castle 20 years ago.
