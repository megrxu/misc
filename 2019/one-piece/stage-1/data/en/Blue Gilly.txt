Blue Gilly is a martial artist from the Longleg Tribe.
He joined as a gladiator at the Corrida Colosseum to compete for the Mera Mera no Mi.
He is an ally of Luffy during the Dressrosa Arc.
After the battle with the Donquixote Pirates at Dressrosa, he became part of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet when he joined the XXX Gym Martial Arts Alliance, which later converted to a pirate crew called the Ideo Pirates.
