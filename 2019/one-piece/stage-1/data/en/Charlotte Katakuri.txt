Charlotte Katakuri is the second son and third child of the Charlotte Family and one of the Three Sweet Commanders of the Big Mom Pirates.
He is also Totto Land's Minister of Flour (粉大臣, Kona Daijin), governing over Komugi Island, and is the elder triplet brother of Daifuku and Oven.
Due to his actions and role, he is the secondary antagonist in the second half of the Whole Cake Island Arc.
