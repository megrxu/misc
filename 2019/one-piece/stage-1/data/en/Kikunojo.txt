Kikunojo of the Lingering Snow is a samurai from Wano Country and a retainer for the Kozuki Family, serving as one of Kozuki Oden's Nine Red Scabbards.
She is also the younger sibling of Izo, and a child of the dancing school head of the Hanayanagi School in Ringo.
After traveling 20 years forward in time, she donned the alias O-Kiku and worked as a tea house poster girl in Okobore Town.
