Charlotte Oven is the fourth son and fifth child of the Charlotte Family and the younger triplet brother of Katakuri and Daifuku.
He is also an officer of the Big Mom Pirates and serves as Totto Land's Minister of Browned Food (こんがり大臣, Kongari Daijin, Viz: "Minister of Baking"), governing over Yakigashi Island.
Due to his actions and role, he is a major antagonist in the Whole Cake Island Arc.
