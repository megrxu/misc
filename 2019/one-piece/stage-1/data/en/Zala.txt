"Poison Spider" Zala, better known by her alias Miss Doublefinger, was the second highest ranking female  agent in the secret organization known as Baroque Works.
As an officer agent, she was partnered with Mr. 1.
However, she also used the alias of Paula while she acted as the owner of Spiders Cafe.
She was a major antagonist during the Alabasta Arc.
After the group's dissolution, she went back to being the owner of the New Spiders Cafe alongside most of her Baroque Works colleagues.
