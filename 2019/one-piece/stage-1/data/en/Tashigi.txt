Captain Tashigi is a Marine officer serving under Vice Admiral Smoker in the base of G-5.
She was originally introduced as Master Chief Petty Officer (曹長, Sōchō) in Loguetown  but was promoted to Ensign (少尉, Shōi) after the events in Alabasta.
During the timeskip, she was promoted to Captain (大佐, Taisa).
