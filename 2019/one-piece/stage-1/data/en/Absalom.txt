Absalom of the Graveyard was the leader of the Zombie Soldiers and Zombie Generals of Thriller Bark prior to its collapse.
He was one of the Mysterious Four and one of the major antagonists of the Thriller Bark Saga.
Sometime during the timeskip, he became a reporter under the alias Absa.
At some point, he was abducted by the Blackbeard Pirates and killed to poach his Devil Fruit powers, with his ability going to Shiryu.
