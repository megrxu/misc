Mont Blanc Cricket is an inhabitant of Jaya, living in a small home behind the facade of a castle.
He is the distant descendant of Mont Blanc Noland and the "Last Boss" (, Rasuto Bosu) of the Saruyama Alliance.
He is an ally of the Straw Hat Pirates during the Jaya Arc, and along with the Saruyama Alliance aided them in getting to Skypiea.
