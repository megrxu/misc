Chinjao, formerly known as "Don Chinjao" and "Chinjao the Drill", is a former pirate, the leader of the Chinjao Family, and the retired 12th leader of the Happo Navy.
He also joined as a gladiator at the Corrida Colosseum to compete for the Mera Mera no Mi.
He has a bounty of 542,000,000.
Although at first he confronted Luffy for his hatred against Garp, he became his ally during the Dressrosa Arc.
