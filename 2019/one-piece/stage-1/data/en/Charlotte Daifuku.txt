Charlotte Daifuku is the third son and fourth child of the Charlotte Family and the middle triplet brother of Katakuri and Oven.
He is also an officer of the Big Mom Pirates and serves as Totto Land's Minister of Beans (豆大臣, Mame Daijin), governing over Poripori Island.
Due to his actions and role, he is a major antagonist in the Whole Cake Island Arc.
