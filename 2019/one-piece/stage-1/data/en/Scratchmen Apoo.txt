Scratchmen Apoo, also known as "Roar of the Sea", is a longarm and an infamous music-themed pirate and captain of the On Air Pirates.
He and his crew originated from the Grand Line.
He is one of twelve pirates who are referred to as the "Worst Generation".
After the timeskip, his bounty has been raised from 198,000,000 to 350,000,000.
Some time during the timeskip, he became a subordinate to the Yonko Kaido.
