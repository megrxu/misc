Ashura Doji is a retainer of the Kozuki Family as one of the Nine Red Scabbards once led by Kozuki Oden.
After Oden's death he returned to being a criminal under the name Shutenmaru, assuming leadership of the Mt.
Atama Thieves.
He initially severed his ties with the Kozuki Family in the present but rejoined after witnessing Yasuie's death.
