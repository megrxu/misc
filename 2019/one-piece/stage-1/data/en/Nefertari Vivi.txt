Nefertari Vivi is the princess of Alabasta.
She is the daughter of Nefertari Cobra and Titi.
She was one of the main antagonists of the Reverse Mountain Arc under the Baroque Works codename Miss Wednesday.
However, she ended up joining the Straw Hat Pirates for most of the Alabasta Saga after revealing herself to be a spy plotting against Baroque Works.
She left the crew after defeating Baroque Works to stay in her home country, but has been shown to be keeping track of the Straw Hat's progress.
