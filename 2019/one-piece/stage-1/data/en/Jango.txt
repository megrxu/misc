The subject of this article is sometimes called Django.
"Double-Crosser" Jango is a hypnotist and Marine seaman recruit under Rear Admiral Hina.
He was formerly the Captain of the Black Cat Pirates and Captain Kuro's first mate.
Due to his actions, Jango can be considered the secondary antagonist of the Syrup Village Arc.
He is also the protagonist of the Jango's Dance Paradise Cover Page Serial.
