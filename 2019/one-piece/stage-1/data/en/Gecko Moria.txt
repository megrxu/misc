Gecko Moria is the captain of the Thriller Bark Pirates and a former Shichibukai who resides on the largest ship in the world, Thriller Bark.
His bounty is 320,000,000, which was frozen until he lost his Shichibukai position.
He is the primary antagonist of the Thriller Bark Saga who stole the shadows of Brook and various other beings with the power of the Kage Kage no Mi.
He is also a major antagonist in the Marineford Arc where he took part in the Battle of Marineford and sided with the Marines, along with most of the other Shichibukai.
After the war's end, he was stripped of his title and was to be eliminated by Donquixote Doflamingo, but he managed to escape.
