"Blood-Splatterer" Coribou is a captain of the Caribou Pirates, along with his brother Caribou.
He is famous for killing Marines and was at the Sabaody Archipelago around the time of the Straw Hat Pirates' reunion.
He, along with Caribou, another infamous rookie, joined the Fake Straw Hat Crew temporarily.
He has a bounty of 190,000,000, making him part of the new generation of rookies, following the path of Luffy and the other Supernovas with bounties over 100,000,000.
