Master Nekomamushi is a feline mink and the guardian of the Whale Forest on Zou, dubbed the "Ruler of Night" as he rules over the Mokomo Dukedom from 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM.
He and Inuarashi serve as retainers for the Kozuki Family of Wano Country, as members of the Nine Red Scabbards.
Alongside Inuarashi, he was once a member of the Whitebeard Pirates and Roger Pirates, serving under Kozuki Oden.Pedro has claimed that he and Inuarashi must not be allowed to die because "the world is waiting for them".
