"Foxfire Kin'emon" is a samurai from Wano Country, a retainer of the Kozuki Family, the leader of the Nine Red Scabbards, the husband of Tsuru, and a former thug.
He posed as Momonosuke's father during the journey to Zou.
He was born 56 years before the present day, but traveled 20 years forward in time when he was 36 years old.
He is one of the central allies of the Straw Hat Pirates during the Dressrosa and Yonko Sagas.
