Lieutenant Commander Helmeppo is a Marine officer and the son of disgraced Marine Captain "Axe-Hand" Morgan.
Once a spoiled child who took advantage of his father's status, he is now a lieutenant commander  who trained with Koby under Vice Admiral Garp.
Due to his actions when he was first introduced, he can be considered a supporting antagonist of the Romance Dawn Arc.
He and Koby also are the central characters from Diary of Koby-Meppo Cover Page Serial.
