Tilestone is one of the five foremen of Dock One of the Galley-La Company in Water 7.
Tilestone is the last of the foremen to be formally introduced in the story, although he is present from the first scene Galley-La is shown in.
His specialties are cabinetry, caulking, and flag making.
He became an ally of the Straw Hat Pirates during the end of the Water 7 Arc and the beginning of the Enies Lobby Arc.
