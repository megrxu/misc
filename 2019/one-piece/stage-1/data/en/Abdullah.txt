Abdullah is a criminal and a former bounty hunter who joined as a gladiator at the Corrida Colosseum to compete for the Mera Mera no Mi, along with his partner Jeet.
He is an ally of Luffy during the Dressrosa Arc.
After the battle with the Donquixote Pirates at Dressrosa, he joined the XXX Gym Martial Arts Alliance, which later converted to a pirate crew called the Ideo Pirates.
