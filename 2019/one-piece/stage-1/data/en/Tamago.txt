Baron Tamago (also known as Viscount Hiyoko and Count Niwatori in his second and third form respectively) is a knight-ranked combatant of the Big Mom Pirates, who originates from the Longleg Tribe.
He and his partner Pekoms are responsible for collecting the monthly payment of sweets from Fish-Man Island in exchange for Big Mom's protection.
Due to his actions and role, he is a supporting antagonist during the Whole Cake Island Arc.
