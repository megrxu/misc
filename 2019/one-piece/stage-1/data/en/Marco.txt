Marco the Phoenix is the former 1st division commander of the Whitebeard Pirates.
Once starting out as an apprentice on the crew, he had come to be Whitebeard's closest confidante and right-hand man.
After his captain's death, Marco assumed leadership of the Whitebeard Pirates until the crew's disbandment following the Payback War.
Since said war during the two-year timeskip, Marco works as a doctor at Whitebeard's home village, Sphinx.
